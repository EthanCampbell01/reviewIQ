# ReviewIQ — Business Plan Summary

## The Problem

24M+ Amazon sellers manually read competitor reviews to find product improvement opportunities. It takes 4–6 hours per product and most sellers don't do it at all — leaving critical intelligence on the table.

## The Solution

ReviewIQ automates the entire process: scrape → AI analysis → actionable report in minutes, updated weekly automatically.

## Go-To-Market

1. **Validate first** — landing page + $100 Facebook ads to Amazon seller audiences
2. **Reddit/Facebook communities** — r/AmazonFBA (400K members), seller groups
3. **Product Hunt launch** at Month 3
4. **Newsletter sponsorships** — Amazon seller newsletters ($500–1K each)
5. **Affiliate programme** — 30% recurring for FBA YouTubers
6. **Free single-ASIN tool** as viral lead magnet

## Competitive Advantage

- Purpose-built for review intelligence (not buried in a 50-feature suite)
- Weekly automated monitoring (not one-off reports)
- Actionable listing copy in buyer language
- Priced for small sellers ($49) not just enterprise
- Historical review trend data compounds over time
